Adjusted Cases:
Cumulative number of confirmed cases but, first recorded patient is set on day one. This allows us to compare which country is doing better than the another.

The effectiveness of a lockdown is also correlatable with the number of cases through the strictness measure provided in the legend.

An interesting comparison is between Malaysia and Canada. These two countries had their first patient in the same week. Currently, Malaysia has a higher strictness rating compared to Canada. They were neck in neck for around the first 50 days. But after that, Canada surpassed Malaysia and the gap kept widening.
